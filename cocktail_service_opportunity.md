## Background

A move from administration to dissolution was filed 4 January 2025, with final dissolution on 4 April 2025. Verified via Companies House on 18 March 2026.

They were one of the UK's leading cocktail and mobile bar hire specialists, operating since 2009 from their Oxford HQ with regional hubs in Edinburgh and London. Services included staffing solutions, campaign activations, luxury mobile bar hire, and drinks consultancy — very similar to HHT's positioning.

Their fixed assets were approximately GBP 105k with current assets around GBP 856k (May 2022 accounts). Net liabilities of approximately GBP 98k at that point suggest the business was already under pressure before entering administration.

**The opportunity:** Their client book is now orphaned. Event planners, spirit brands, and agencies that relied on The Cocktail Service now need a replacement provider. HHT is the natural successor given Joe's consultancy-grade credentials and similar service offering.

**Specific offer to lead with:**

## Orphaned Client #2: Pub in the Park

### What It Is
Pub in the Park was a travelling food and music festival founded by chef Tom Kerridge, visiting multiple UK towns including Marlow, Bath, Tunbridge Wells, Dulwich, St Albans, and Chiswick. Each event featured top restaurants, live music, and drinks activations.

### Current Status — CAUTION
**Critical finding:** The domain pubinthepark.com has expired and is for sale on HugeDomains. Brand Events (the organiser's company) website brandevents.co.uk shows a default Plesk server page. This strongly suggests the festival may have ceased operations or is in extended hiatus.

**Recommendation:** Do NOT invest time pursuing this until verifying the festival's status. Check:
1. Instagram @pubintheparkuk — is there recent activity?
2. Tom Kerridge's own channels — has he announced 2026 dates?
3. Industry contacts — does Joe know anyone in Tom Kerridge's network?

### If the Festival Is Still Running
The Cocktail Service ran cocktail bars at multiple Pub in the Park dates. If the festival resumes, the organiser will need a replacement.

**Pitch angle:** Emphasise HHT's ability to deliver at scale across multiple touring dates. Joe's personal credibility in the bar world (Milk & Honey, Trailer Happiness) would resonate with a Tom Kerridge-branded festival that values craft credentials.

**If the festival is dead:** The brand may be acquired or relaunched. Monitor only — don't chase.
