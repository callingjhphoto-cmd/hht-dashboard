# HHT Platform Demo Script — For Joe Stokoe
**Duration:** 5 minutes max
**Format:** Screen share (Zoom/WhatsApp video)
**Presenter:** James
**Goal:** Get Joe to say "yes, let's use this" — then discuss retainer

---

## Pre-Demo Checklist
- [ ] Operations platform loaded: https://callingjhphoto-cmd.github.io/hht-operations-platform/
- [ ] Intelligence dashboard loaded: https://callingjhphoto-cmd.github.io/hht-dashboard/
- [ ] Have joe_alert_2026-03-18.md open (Cocktail Service orphaned clients)
- [ ] Phone on silent, clean desktop

---

## OPENING (30 seconds)

> "Joe, I've built something I think you'll find useful. You mentioned looking at hiring someone for lead gen — I want to show you what I've put together before you commit to that. It's a platform specifically for HHT that does lead generation, competitor tracking, and pipeline management. Five minutes."

**Key frame:** This isn't a cost play. This is "I built you a machine that finds leads while you sleep."

---

## SCREEN 1: Lead Engine CRM (90 seconds) — THE MONEY SHOT

Open the operations platform → Lead Engine tab.

**Show:**
1. **The map** — "392 real UK venues already loaded. These are wedding venues, corporate event spaces, country houses — all verified against real directory listings."
2. **Click a county** — show venue cards with details
3. **Classification tags** — "Each venue is tagged: dry hire, external bar allowed, or in-house only. We filter out the ones you can't work with. You only see leads worth calling."
4. **Lead scoring** — "Every lead gets a score out of 100. Score 80+? You get an alert. Those are the ones to call first."

> "Right now, 392 venues. The scraper runs weekly and adds new ones automatically. No one has to do anything — it just keeps finding leads."

**Don't say:** "AI" repeatedly. Joe wants results, not tech buzzwords.

---

## SCREEN 2: Intelligence Dashboard (90 seconds) — THE EDGE

Switch to the intelligence dashboard.

**Show:**
1. **Lead Pipeline tab** — venue breakdown by county, lead quality distribution
2. **Competitor Tracking tab** — "58 competitors mapped. Every mobile cocktail company in the UK. I know who they are, where they operate, their Instagram, their pricing tier."
3. **Scroll the full competitor table** — let it sink in. 58 rows.
4. **Cotswolds gap insight** — "Only 6 competitors serve the Cotswolds and Oxfordshire. Most are mid-tier or budget. There's no premium operator there. That's your gap."

> "Your competitors don't have this intelligence. They're not tracking each other. You will be."

---

## SCREEN 3: The Cocktail Service Opportunity (60 seconds) — THE HOOK

This is where Joe leans in. He knows The Cocktail Service.

> "You know The Cocktail Service went under. I've already mapped their orphaned clients. Three big ones: **Pub in the Park** — Tom Kerridge's festival, runs in Marlow, Bath, Tunbridge Wells, St Albans. **Blenheim Palace** — premium weddings, Oxfordshire. **BBC Good Food Show** — NEC Birmingham, Olympia London. All three currently have no bar supplier. These are warm leads, not cold calls."

Show the joe_alert file or just state the data verbally.

> "The system flagged these automatically because of the competitor intelligence layer. That's the kind of thing a salesperson doesn't catch."

---

## SCREEN 4: Pipeline & KPIs (30 seconds) — THE PROFESSIONAL TOUCH

Quick flash of:
- Command Centre — revenue KPIs, activity feed
- Event Pipeline — 7-stage Kanban (Lead Captured through Post-Event Nurture)
- Mention deal rotting: "If a lead goes cold for 14 days, it goes red. Nothing falls through the cracks."

> "Everything in one place. No Dropbox. No separate CRM subscription. Just this."

---

## CLOSE (30 seconds)

> "So that's where it's at. 392 venues loaded, 58 competitors tracked, the Cocktail Service clients flagged. The scraper adds new leads every week. I'd suggest we get your real event data in here and your team starts using it. What do you think?"

**Then shut up and let Joe respond.**

> "Nothing monthly for software — it's your platform, hosted on your account. What I'd propose is a retainer for me to keep the scrapers running, add new features, and maintain the data. We can talk numbers, but it's a fraction of a salesperson."

**"Can I try it?"**
> "It's live right now. I'll send you the links. Play with it."

**"What about my current CRM?"**
> "This replaces it. Or runs alongside it — your call. But the lead gen and intelligence layer are things no CRM gives you."

**"Is the data real?"**
> "Every venue is from a real directory listing — Guides for Brides, Google Maps, Hitched, Bridebook. The competitor profiles are from their actual websites and Instagram. Nothing made up."

**"What about Home Counties / Cotswolds specifically?"**
> "That's exactly where the scraper is focused. Oxfordshire, Berkshire, Buckinghamshire, Gloucestershire, Hampshire, Surrey, Hertfordshire, Wiltshire. Country houses without in-house catering are flagged as gold — those are the venues that need you."
