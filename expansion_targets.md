# Outside London Expansion — Target Venues

**Prepared for:** Joe Stokoe / Heads, Hearts & Tails
**Date:** 19 March 2026
**Regions:** Home Counties, Oxfordshire, Cotswolds

---

## Strategy

Joe wants to expand beyond London into the Home Counties, Oxfordshire, and Cotswolds. These regions have the highest concentration of dry-hire wedding venues, country houses, and barn conversions in England — exactly the venues that need external cocktail bar services.

**Why these areas work for HHT:**
- High average wedding spend (Oxfordshire/Cotswolds average GBP 35-45k per wedding)
- Majority of country house and barn venues are dry-hire or external-caterer-only
- Less competition from premium mobile bar companies (most competitors are London-centric)
- Good driving distance from London (1-2 hours) — Joe can service these without regional staff initially
- The Cocktail Service was Oxford-based — their local clients are now unserved
